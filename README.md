# Customer-Segmentation-Project
Customer Segmentation using K-Means clustering to group customers based on income and spending behavior. Provides insights for targeted marketing and better customer understanding through data visualization.

##  Project Overview
Customer Segmentation is a data analytics project that groups customers based on their demographic information and purchasing behavior. The objective is to identify distinct customer groups and provide actionable business insights that help improve customer retention, marketing effectiveness, and revenue generation.

## Business Objective
Businesses often serve customers with different spending habits and preferences. Applying the same marketing strategy to all customers may reduce efficiency and profitability.
The primary objective of this project is to:
- Identify different customer groups.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Improve targeted marketing campaigns.
- Increase customer retention and engagement.
- Support data-driven business decisions.
